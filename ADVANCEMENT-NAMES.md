## 🏆 PROJECT DESCRIPTION FEATURES

Based on your description: *"Security & Bureau of Cyber Offenders are masking themselves with Proxy and VPN services. Police Solution: Scan and detect given IP address (IPv4/IPv6) is Original or Proxy/VPN enabled and fetch WHOIS Records"*

### **What Our Project Does:**

✅ **VPN/Proxy Detection** - Identifies if criminals are hiding behind anonymization services  
✅ **IPv4/IPv6 Support** - Scans both IP address formats  
✅ **Original vs Masked Classification** - Distinguishes legitimate IPs from proxy/VPN IPs  
✅ **WHOIS Intelligence** - Fetches complete network ownership records  
✅ **Real-time Threat Scoring** - 0-100 risk assessment with 5 threat levels  
✅ **Bulk Surveillance** - Process multiple suspect IPs from CSV files  
✅ **Evidence Export** - CSV export for court reports and documentation  
✅ **Audit Trail** - Complete detection history for investigations  
✅ **Multi-factor Verification** - 7+ detection methods for accuracy  
✅ **Law Enforcement Dashboard** - Real-time analytics and statistics
